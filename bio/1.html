Chapter 1: Cells

I.  Introduction

Cells are the basic definition of life.
They have all the characteristics of life.

  • Singly —as unicellular organisms
  • As part of multicellular organisms

1.2    Longitudinal v.s. Transverse section

Longitudinal — cutting along the length of the cell
Transverse — cutting across the length of the cell
