I. Local communities and nearby nature areas are mutually dependent on each other

  • Humans and nature are dependent on each other
  • They bring about benefits and disadvantages to each other.

The relationship between people and nature

Humans dependent on nature

  1. Humans depend on nature for their survival
    • Nature provides air and water.
    • Bees help to pollinate crops for a continuous supply of food.
  2. Nature provides spaces for recreation
    • Allows people to bond with family and friends
    • Lets people improve their physical and mental well-being.
  3. Nature lowers temperatures and provides shade.
  4. Nature removes pollutants.

Nature dependent on humans
