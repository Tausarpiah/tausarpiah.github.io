Purpose

  • Purpose is impact
  • How would the [author] want the audience/reader to feel?
  • How the audience to react and what would the audience be likely to do?
Understanding how to identify purpose is important in doing comparisons and reliability. Also being able to write out correctly the purpose of each source will yield the student higher marks in the source-based question.
