SEQ

Why SEQ -- requres explainig why a particular event or issue happened.
  • Provide 2 distinct reasonings
  • Explain why...

SEQ Marking scheme

L1 (1-2 marks) Describe events without linking
L2 (3-4 marks) Describe issue/event/reason
1 detailed description = 3 marks
2 detailed descriptions = 4 marks
L3 (5-8 marks) Explain 2 issues/events/reasons
1 explained issue = 5-6 marks
2 explained issues = 7-8 marks

Explanation

  1. Give variety of explanations – Sometimes 203 types of exp are needed
  2. MUST ALWAYS LINK BACK TO WHAT THE QUESTION IS ASKING FOR
  3. Outcome – e.g. outcome = hated. All elaboration must be headed towards explaing this outcome keywords (e.g. outcome kw = hated — all elab must link to the word 'hated')
  4. Examples – specific or general
